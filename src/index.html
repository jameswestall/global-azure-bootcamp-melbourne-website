Agentic AI: the future of innovation or just hype?

Who tests their cloud code anyway?

If you are a developer, you know as well as me that testing your code is optional. And when we suddenly involve cloud computing, there are even more excuses not to test your applications and infrastructure. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, a LEGO fan, or a parent looking to inspire your kids, this session is all about exploring the fun side of AI and robotics. Building Responsible AI Apps with Azure AI Foundry

In this session we’ll learn how to build responsible AI-powered apps using Azure’s AI Foundry. We’ll cover everything from comparing and selecting models, running evaluations, implementing safety mechanisms, deploying and monitoring your app and automating it all with CI/CD from dev to production. This session has something for everyone from the complete beginner to those who have experimented with AI apps all the way to those who have built them using other tools and services.

The Future of Cloud Security: AI, Zero Trust, and Beyond

Cloud security is evolving at an unprecedented pace, driven by AI-powered cyber threats, cloud-native architectures, and increasingly sophisticated attacks. To stay ahead, organisations should embrace Zero Trust principles while leveraging AI-driven security solutions. This session explores the interconnection of AI and Zero Trust in modern cloud security, highlighting how AI enhances Zero Trust frameworks and how Microsoft provides an ecosystem for next generation of security architectures.

Embracing the Dapr Transactional Outbox with WebAPIs on Azure Container Apps

In distributed systems, the dual-write problem can lead to inconsistent states when a database update and a messaging operation don't sync perfectly. Join me as I share real-world scenarios and code samples showing how Dapr’s Transactional Outbox in ASP.NET Core WebAPI running on Azure Container Apps tackles this challenge. Learn when this pattern is a game changer—and when you might not need it. Let's make every transaction count!

By the end of this session, you'll understand how KubeFleet and AKS Fleet Manager work, and how you can use them to manage your AKS clusters more effectively.

Revolutionising DevOps with AI: From Pipelines to Deployment

In the rapidly evolving world of technology, the fusion of AI and DevOps is transforming how we build, test, and deploy applications. Join us for an engaging and insightful session where we delve into the cutting-edge integration of AI in DevOps pipelines. In this session, we will explore: - Building AI-Powered DevOps Pipelines: Discover how to leverage AI to enhance your DevOps workflows, automate repetitive tasks, and improve efficiency. - Integrating Lint Tests: Learn the importance of linting in maintaining code quality and how AI can streamline the process by integrating smart lint tests into your pipeline. - Deploying AI Infrastructure and Models: Gain insights into deploying scalable and secure AI infrastructure on Azure, ensuring your AI models perform optimally in production environments. - Adding PSRule for Azure: Understand the role of PSRule in enforcing governance and compliance standards within your Azure environment, and how to integrate it seamlessly into your DevOps practices.

01:40pm - 02:25pm

Importance of evaluating Gen AI security and safety guardrails

GenAIOps focuses on establishing a reliable and repeatable process for managing generative AI applications throughout their lifecycle. Evaluation is crucial at every stage, from selecting the base model to pre-production testing and ongoing post-production monitoring. By systematically assessing and addressing risks and refining AI systems at each step, teams can develop generative AI solutions that are not only powerful but also dependable and safe for real-world deployment. Azure AI Foundry offers a robust platform for developing and managing generative AI applications. Ensuring the security and safety of these applications is paramount, and this bootcamp session aims to provide participants with in-depth knowledge and hands-on experience in evaluating and mitigating potential risks. By attending this session, participants will gain valuable insights into the best practices for implementing secure and safe generative AI applications by using content safety filters in user input prompt and llm output. They will leave with a clear understanding of why it is important to evaluate guardrails continously; and also how to achieve it using a systematic evaluation process in Azure AI Foundry.

Network Security for AI Design: The Good, The Bad and The ugly

We will unravel the mystery of how these services connect to each other, explore the various options available, and discuss security preferences. Drawing from our multi-year experience working with security-sensitive workloads, we will include insights on Azure OpenAI, AKS, Azure Function, API Management, Fabric and other unique services.
